# Thaileng Thol || M.eng (Water Resource Engineer)

Hydrologist and Water Engineer with over 10 years of experience across Cambodia, Thailand, Denmark, and Laos PDR, specializing in hydrological and hydrodynamic modeling, rainfall-runoff systems, and real-time river forecasting. Proficient in a broad suite of modeling tools — including WRF, HEC-RAS, MIKE Urban, and SWAT — with hands-on experience integrating satellite remote sensing, radar data, and numerical weather prediction into operational forecasting systems. Has contributed to peer-reviewed publications and open-source tools (PyAEZ) through collaborations with FAO, UNEP, GGGI, DHI, and AIT. Experienced in delivering technical training and capacity building programs for government staff across the Asia-Pacific region. Committed to advancing data-driven approaches in water resources management, climate adaptation, and sustainable development.
